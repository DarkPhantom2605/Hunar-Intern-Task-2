Available Courses

BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)

BCA is a 3-year undergraduate degree course in computer applications. Ideal for students interested in computer programming, web development, and software engineering.

BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)

BBA is a 3-year undergraduate degree that prepares students for management and business roles. This course includes lessons on business operations, marketing, and finance.

MCA (Master of Computer Applications)

MCA is a 2-year postgraduate course focused on advanced topics in computer science, including software development, database management, and machine learning.

MBA (Master of Business Administration)

MBA is a 2-year postgraduate degree that emphasizes leadership, strategic thinking, and decision-making skills necessary for managing businesses at a high level.
