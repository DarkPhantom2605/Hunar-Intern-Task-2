About Us

The Institute of Management and Planning & Advanced Computer Training (IMPACT) College, affiliated to Aryabhatta Knowledge University, Patna was founded by the group leaders in 2014 and has been carving out a niche for itself as the leading institution in Bihar in terms of imparting quality education to undergraduate students, pursuing Bachelor in Business Management (BBA), Bachelor in Computer Application (BCA), Bachelor in Commerce (Professional) (BCP) and B.A. (Journalism & Mass Communication) BA(JMC).
